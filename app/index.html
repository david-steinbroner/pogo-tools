ABOUT POGO PAL
POGO PAL helps you make smart choices when you BATTLE, COLLECT, and TRADE your POKéMON.
VERSUS
Recommendations are based on the POKéMON type chart. Each TYPE has strengths (super effective) and weaknesses (not very effective).

When you select opponent types, POGO PAL calculates:

• Offensive advantage: which MOVE TYPES deal the most damage
• Defensive advantage: which POKéMON TYPES receive the least damage
CSV UPLOAD
Tap to upload an accepted CSV format of your POKéMON, and POGO PAL will recommend your actual POKéMON to bring and not bring to each BATTLE.

Note: only CSVs downloaded from PokeGenie are accepted at the moment.

POGO PAL SAYS
When battling, moves matter most. Damage comes from a MOVE TYPE of the POKéMON, not the POKéMON itself.
POKéMON usually learn moves matching their own TYPE, so bringing a FIRE-TYPE against GRASS-TYPE usually means you'll have the right moves to do the most damage.
That said, CP matters too: a high-CP POKéMON with the "wrong" MOVE TYPE will often beat a low-CP POKéMON with the "right" MOVE TYPE.
